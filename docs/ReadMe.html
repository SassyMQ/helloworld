There are 5 messages in this lexicon sent by 3 different actors who speak and 3 actors who can hear messages.

Programmer

Programmer can say

  1. "Hello" to World in the general channel.
  2. "Goodbye" to World in the general channel.
  3. "Get All Galaxies" to World in the general channel.

Programmer can hear

  1. "Whats Up" from World.

World

World can say

  1. "Whats Up" to Programmer in the general channel.

World can hear

  1. "Hello" from Programmer.
  2. "Goodbye" from Programmer.
  3. "Get All Galaxies" from Programmer.
  4. "Yo Yo" from Universe.

World can also hear everything that Programmer hears

Universe

Universe can say

  1. "Yo Yo" to World in the general channel.

Universe can hear

    Universe can also hear everything that World hears
